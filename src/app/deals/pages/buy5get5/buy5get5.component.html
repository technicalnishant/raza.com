FAQ’S
Once I purchase a calling plan, how do I place my first call?

Upon purchasing a calling plan you will receive a confirmation Email providing you with a local access number to dial out.

1. Use your registered phone and dial the local access number.

2. Dial 011+ Country code + Destination number to get connected.

3. Keep Talking!

Why are the rates different when calling a cell phone?

In most non-domestic countries, a surcharge is applied when calling a Mobile phone. This additional charge results in rate increases. However, for certain countries it is cheaper to call Mobiles than landlines.
