I lost my password, how do I reset? add remove

Click “Forgot Password” link on the login page to receive your password by Text or E-mail.

Calling
Once I purchase a calling plan, how do i place my first call? add remove

Upon purchasing a calling plan you will receive a confirmation Email providing you with a local access number to dial out.

1. Use your registered phone and dial the local access number.

2. Dial 011+ Country code + Destination number to get connected.

3. Keep Talking!

 Can I use my calling plan to dial any country other than what I selected while sign up. add remove

Yes, however the rates advertised on the web only apply to the specific country of selection. Please visit our “search rate” page to view all rates.

Billing/Payment
What method of payments is accepted? add remove

We accept almost all major Credit and Debit cards from USA and only Visa Debit from CANADA. Payments are also accepted using PayPal however few discounted plans may not be eligible to use PayPal as payment method.

How do I make the payment? add remove

Payments can be made using our Automated system, Raza Mobile Applications, Website, Live chat help or by calling our 24/7 customer service.
